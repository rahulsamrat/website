Welcome to Stable Isotope Laboratory



The journey of SILIKA started with installation of GC-MS-MS and ASE 350 in 2012. Subsequently, MAT 253 was installed along with Gas Bench II, KEIL carbonate IV, GC-TC and Elemental Analyser.
SILIKA offers analysis for stable isotopes of H, C, O, N and S in a wide variety of samples. Most samples can be efficiently analyzed in continuous flow mode in MAT 253. Dual inlet mode is used for very high precision work and for measuring isotopic ratios in samples processed off-line. For oragnic samples, before measuring the isotopic ratios, SILIKA has facility of characterising organic componds by GC-MS-MS,GC-FID and Pyrolyser.
